Lasagna

A pasta cake basically. Beloved by a certain grumpy orange cat that hates monday, and also beloved by me, who also hates mondays.

Ingredients:

  • pasta plates
  • cheese
  • ground meat
  • tomato sauce
  • flour
  • milk
  • spices

Process:

  1. Make meat sauce.
  2. Make bechamel sauce.
  3. Assemble.
  4. Bake.

Tips&tricks:

  • Put some cold tomato sauce over a hot lasagna.
  • Bake it with aluminum foil until the last 20 minutes, then remove the foil to get some extra crunch on top.
