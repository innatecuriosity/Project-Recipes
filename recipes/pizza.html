Pizza

We all know it, one of the most iconic dishes in modern culture. A piece of flatbread, usually circular, with various toppings, that should at least include cheese and tomato sauce. It's not really healthy on a phyisical level, but can do wonders for our mental health. And as I heard from a neuroscientist once (no joke) "One has to decide every now and then, do I want to be healthy, or do I want to be happy"

Ingredients:

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • other toppings
  • spices

Process:

  1. Spread the dough.
  2. Cover with tomato sauce.
  3. Add toppings.
  4. Bake

Tips&tricks:

  • Make enough so you can eat cold leftovers in the morning.
